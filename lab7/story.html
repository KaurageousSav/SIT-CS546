My fantasy office wear

Back when there was no pandemic in the face of earth, I dreamt of going to office as if I am going to be crowned next imaginary 'Miss Office' title.
For this, I bought all sort of necessary head to toe accessories and clothes. It was exactly after three weeks of this huge purchase that we had an absolute lockdown because of the pandemic and I never got to the see the face of office again.

Till today, I couldnt really utilize those purchases to their full worth and as far as clothes are concerned, I have grown two sizes out of them.
